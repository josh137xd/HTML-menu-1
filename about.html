Origin Story

Our flagship location is in New York, NY. The owner, John, always dreamed of opening a restaurant that served authentic Italian cuisine. He grew up in Rome and learned the art of Italian cooking from his nonna. When he moved to the United States, he was disappointed by the lack of authentic Italian restaurants. So, he decided to open his own restaurant and bring a taste of Rome to the Big Apple.
